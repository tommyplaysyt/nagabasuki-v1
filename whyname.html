Why We Chose Our Name

Our name, Naga Basuki Ink, carries deep meaning and represents the essence of our artistry. Here's the story behind it:

Meaning of "Naga Basuki"

The name "Naga Basuki" is rooted in Balinese culture and mythology. "Naga" translates to dragon or serpent, symbolising strength and protection. "Basuki" means blessing or good fortune. Together, "Naga Basuki" embraces a powerful guardian who presents blessings and prosperity.

Our Design Philosophy

Inspired by our name, our designs merge traditional Balinese art with modern elements, creating tattoos that tell a personal story. We aim to provide a unique and meaningful experience, blending body art with cultural significance.
